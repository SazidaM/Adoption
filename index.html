Meet Leo!

Leo is an adorable American Shorthair with a big personality and even bigger heart. Known for his stubborn streak, Leo always knows what he wants-- but don't let him fool you! Behind his determined nature lies a protective and caring companion who will always have your back. With his soft fur, captivating eyes, and a knack for looking irresistibly cute, Leo is truly one of a kind. Whether he's lounging in a sunny spot or standing guard, Leo is ready to bring both charm and security to his forever home.

Habitat

Leo thrives in a cozy, quiet home where he can relax and enjoy his favorite pastimes. He loves spending his afternoons perched by a sunny window, watching the birds and passerby with keen interest. While he enjoys his independence, Leo also appreciates having a few cozy quiet spots where he can retreat for some quiet time. Leo's adaptable nature ensures he'll settle in comfortably--as long as he has a sunny window and a warm lap nearby!
